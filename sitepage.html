Game Description

Dec 20, 2020 STAR PIRATES is a survival based game where you and a clan of friends fight to survive the unforgiving life in space. Earth has fallen and with it many empires after it have also collapsed due to rebels, so pirates run free among the stars pillaging and raiding enemy ships and land, in order to establish control and power for themselves.In STAR PIRATES, you must choose a character and build a ship type that fits you and your crews needs, whether its speed you need or raw power, the shipbuilder has it all.
